What is the Bosnian Fest?

Our community is hosting this festival to give the opportunity for you to have first hand experience with Bosnian food, music, dancing, and culture. The event will give free entry, only charging for food and drinks.

We will be providing you an opportunity to taste both savory and sweet traditional Bosnian dishes that are food stapes from back home.

We will also have a program for you to witness Bosnian folk dances and music/musical performances.

There will be a lot historical and cultural information on where we come from and who we are.
